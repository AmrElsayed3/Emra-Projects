Lee Min Ho

  • Name: Lee Min Ho
  • Native name: 이민호
  • Nationality: South Korean
  • Gender: Male
  • Born: June 22, 1987
  • Age: 32
Lee Min Ho is a South Korean actor and singer. He is best known for his leading roles in "Boys Over Flowers", "City Hunter", "The Heirs" and "The Legend of the Blue Sea". In his second year of high school, Lee started acting. By his senior year in high school, he had joined "Starhaus Entertainment" with the help of an acquaintance. After going through training, Lee started auditioning and landed small roles in several television dramas. His breakthrough came in 2009 with the lead role of Gu Jun Pyo in Boys Over Flowers. In July 2012, Lee was chosen as the most attractive male celebrity in TV commercials for cosmetics brands. Lee Min Ho and Suzy were revealed to be dating on March 22, 2015. However, on November 16, 2017, it was reported that the couple split.

On March 15, 2018 Lee entered his military training at the Korea Army Training Centre in Nonsan, South Chungcheong Province. He returned to his public service duty after four weeks of basic military training. He was discharged on April 25, 2019

(Source: Wikipedia)
